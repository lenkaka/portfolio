App Design for Mobile&Tablet - FWD Cube

 

Project Brief

This is an All-in-one super app for optimising seller experience and sales managements for improving agents' daily workflow. It is designed and customised for different regions and countries, including Thailand, Philippine, Japan, Malaysia and so on, which will be based on their different cultures, working behaviors and business needs.

Target Users

Users of all ages from the company that will use this app to do the sales activities, manage leads and customers, manage teammates, and other related activities. How they use the app varies from different countries and cultures

Target Audience Study

There are different workflows for different countries based on their different culture.

For Thailand/ Philippine/ Malaysia

  • Managers mainly use CUBE for managing their teammates, leads and customers
  • Agents mainly use CUBE for managing their daily tasks, policies, applications, leads and customers

For Japan

  • Managers mainly use CUBE for managing their solicitors(teammates)
  • Solicitors mainly use CUBE for taking notes for meeting and arranging meetings with agents from different sites, to know about their activities for their customers
