About Me

Having a solid education background in industrial design for 4 years, I was engaged in other design fields such as multimedia design, user experience design and user interface design. I am a creative, adaptable and bright designer with a sensitive sense for details and design.

Besides, I also had experience in project executive to take over the whole project and make it run well till finished design.

What I like most about my design path is that with working in different types/industries of company, it allows me get close to the most popular trend in the world - user experience and Internet. Moreover, I can know more about what users think and find out what they really need. The most valuable thing is to design the best solutions for them to use the product with a good experience.

What I think in my mind and what I do with my hands are much more than what I can say.
