Harmony

National Top 35 in 8th Vatti Industrial Design Competition, 2013

“Harmony” is an innovative concept design for movable kitchen to let 2 people have more space working in the kitchen. I was inspired by my parents' cooking in a small kitchen and they were always blamed for each other. This design breaks the limit of traditional kitchen pattern. When one use it, he can place it on one side. When two people use it, the movable part can be place in the middle, thus, there will have enough space for both two people. Moreover, raw food and dishes can be placed on the movable part conveniently. This project unanimously got great appraisal of the Evaluation Committee and I was nominated for the national top 35 participants.
