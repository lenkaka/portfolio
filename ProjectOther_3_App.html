Coffee Pet - Game design with AR (Augmented Reality) Technology

After scanning the coaster by using smart phones, we can play with virtual 3D pets. By raising them, cats can be dressed up playing games. We can also build up their own image as well as form a family with pets of others', which is related to self-esteem and social communication according to psychology. On the other hand, in the business part for cafes, this game is also a marketing tool, which can increase purchase and help cafe build brand.
